Interesting stuff about pandas.

The panda is a distinctive black-and-white bear native to the bamboo forests of southwest China. It spends majority of its day eating bamboo, which makes up more than 99% of its diet. Despite being classified as a carnivore, the panda has adapted to a herbivorous lifestyle. Pandas are known for their gentle temperament and solitary nature. They have a unique thumb-like appendage that helps them grasp bamboo stalks. Conservation efforts have helped increase the panda population, but they are still considered a vulnerable species due to habitat loss and low birth rates.
